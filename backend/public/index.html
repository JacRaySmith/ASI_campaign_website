Candidate statement

As a Computer Science student at Cal State LA, I have sat through lectures completely disconnected from the career I actually want, and I know I'm not alone.

The Academic Senate has direct authority over curriculum, and I intend to exercise it. I will push for student-driven curriculum reform, informed by student feedback, to ensure our classes build real, marketable skills rather than just satisfy credit requirements.

I cannot do this without knowing your experience. That is why I am building a platform where students from across all departments can share their academic frustrations directly with me.

I am a student like you who wants to feel like my education is shaping me for the life I actually want. Vote for me, and I will make sure your voice shapes the curriculum you are paying for.
